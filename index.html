About Aesha Resources

Aesha Resources is an India-based international procurement, sourcing, supply, import-export and business development company serving the global chemical and pharmaceutical industries.

We connect customers with reliable manufacturers and suppliers through ethical business practices, technical expertise and long-term partnerships. Our objective is to simplify procurement, identify dependable sourcing solutions and create sustainable value for customers, suppliers and manufacturing partners.

Founded by Mr. Ruchir Shah, B.E. (Chemical), SVNIT Surat, MBA (Marketing), Aesha Resources combines nearly 25 years of chemical industry experience spanning procurement, sourcing, supply chain management, international trade and business development.

Our expertise covers pharmaceutical intermediates, APIs, industrial chemicals, specialty chemicals, petrochemicals, fine chemicals and custom sourcing solutions for customers worldwide.
